PEER PRESSURE is a feeling that one must do the the same thing as other people of one's age and social group in order to be liked or respected by them.
For example;If you are with friends who are doing something that you typically would not do and they convince you to do what they are doing.
So this web page is for giving Information like some examples of negative peer pressure and positive peer pressure so as some strategies that can help handle negative peer pressure.
MY INTENTION to exchange this information is because I have seen people who are facing this pressure and they end up comitting suicide because they don't have ways to handling it.
